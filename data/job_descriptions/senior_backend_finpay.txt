Senior Backend Engineer — FinPay (Series B Fintech)

About FinPay:
FinPay is a Series B fintech startup building real-time payment infrastructure for cross-border transactions. We process $2B+ in annual volume across 40 countries.

Role:
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to design, build, and operate core payment processing systems. You will work on high-throughput, low-latency distributed systems that handle millions of transactions daily.

Requirements:
- 5+ years of backend engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and/or Go
- Deep experience with ETL pipelines and data processing at scale
- Hands-on with AWS services (ECS, Lambda, SQS, DynamoDB, S3)
- Production experience with PostgreSQL and Redis
- Familiarity with event-driven architectures (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
- Experience building and operating microservices in containerized environments (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform)

Nice to Have:
- Experience in fintech or payments domain
- Knowledge of PCI-DSS compliance
- AWS Solutions Architect certification (SAA)
- Experience with GraphQL APIs
- Contributions to open-source projects

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement payment processing microservices handling 10,000+ TPS
- Build and maintain ETL pipelines for transaction reconciliation and reporting
- Collaborate with the data team on real-time fraud detection infrastructure
- Participate in on-call rotation and incident response
- Mentor junior engineers and conduct code reviews
- Write technical design documents and ADRs
